About Us

PolyPet is an educational technology platform designed for all students. Our mission is to increase student engagement in foreign language education through interactive pet gamification.

Students care for a virtual pet by completing language lessons, quizzes, and flashcards. This approach increases motivation, retention, and consistent practice.

Target Market

Language learners and schools seeking engaging supplemental language tools.

Business Model

Subscription-based educational platform for schools and families.
